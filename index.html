⚖️ WeightCrown - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Overview

WeightCrown is a single-player medieval decision-making game that respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Game Data Only: WeightCrown stores your game progress, decisions, and kingdom statistics locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your current game progress and scenario completions
  • Kingdom statistics (economy, military, culture points)
  • Decision history within the game
  • App settings and preferences

No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location data.

How We Use Your Information

All game data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing game functionality and saving your progress
  • Determining which scenarios and crises you encounter
  • Maintaining game state between app sessions

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using secure React Native storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Your game progress and decisions remain private to you and stored only on your device.

Advertising

WeightCrown does not contain any advertisements or third-party tracking.

Children's Privacy

WeightCrown does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app contains mature themes and is intended for mature audiences.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your game data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Reset Game" option within the app settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date above and will be made available through the app store listing.
